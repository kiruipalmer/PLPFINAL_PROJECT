How HomeRaft Works

HomeRaft is designed to simplify the process of finding your next rental property. Whether you're looking for a single room, a bedsitter, or a multi-bedroom house, our platform filters the listings to match your preferences.

  • Step 1: Use the search bar to filter properties by location, house type, and management type.
  • Step 2: Browse through the listings to find properties that match your needs.
  • Step 3: Get detailed information about the property, including pricing, deposit, and management contact information.
  • Step 4: Contact the property owner or manager directly to schedule a visit or make a booking.

Our goal is to provide a hassle-free and transparent rental experience for tenants by showcasing properties from trusted agencies and owners. You can also rate your experience with landlords or agencies to help future renters make informed decisions.

Types of Houses

Single Room

A small room for one person, often with shared amenities like a bathroom or kitchen.

Bedsitter

A single room unit with a combined living and sleeping area, usually with a small kitchenette. The bathroom may be private or shared.

1 Bedroom

A one-bedroom apartment typically includes a living room, kitchen, bathroom, and one separate bedroom.

2 Bedroom

A two-bedroom apartment has two separate bedrooms, along with a living room, kitchen, and bathroom, offering more space for small families or roommates.

3 Bedroom

A larger apartment with three separate bedrooms, suitable for larger families or groups of roommates. Includes a kitchen, living room, and bathroom(s).

Penthouse

A luxury apartment located on the top floor of a building, often with additional features like large terraces, expansive views, and high-end finishes.
